FAQs

How is subscription better than a regular agency or full-time designer?

With a subscription, you get the best of both worlds.

Unlike a regular agency, you avoid fluctuating costs and lengthy contracts while enjoying faster turnaround times and unlimited design requests.

Compared to a full-time designer, who can cost $60,000-$100,000 per year with benefits and expenses like trainings, office, 401k, vacations etc., or agency rates of $100-$150 per hour, our subscription model offers the same expertise and flexibility at a fraction of the cost.

It’s a flexible, efficient, and budget-friendly way to get high-quality designs whenever you need them.

How to make design requests?

Once you subscribe, you'll get access to your own Trello board where you can add new design tasks, check on progress, and collaborate seamlessly. Just add a new card with design brief (it could be a google doc, a loom video, just text with description or anything else that suits you) and we'll get to work.

If there will be any question from our end (and there probably will), we'll comment on the card or set up a quick call, if it's a large request.

All this process will be explained in detail during onboarding!

How do i receive designs?

In your Trello board, each card with a request that you add has a comment section where communication around this request will be happening. That's where we'll update you on the progress, ask questions, receive your feedback and notify you when your design is complete.

You'll have your own Figma file (a software we use for design) with all the designs and a Google Drive where all approved files will be uploaded and stored.

Can i hire you to design for multiple clients for my agency?

Definitely! We love to work with agencies. Our subscription is not limited to the amount of your clients, we can work with them all under a single subscription, completing one or two requests at a time, depending on your plan.

How does pausing/cancelling subscription work?

You can pause your subscription at any time, for as long as you want and for any reason. You won't be charged for unused days.

Come back whenever you you're ready again and we'll resume your subscription.

For cancelling, you can just cancel your subscription whenever you decide to stop working with us. No long-term commitment.

Can all types of requsts be completed in a 24-48h?

Usually, most requests take less than two days to complete. However, more complex requests may require more time. We often split such requests into stages and update you on them every working day.

Is there any design work you don't do?

Yes. As our focus is on marketing, we don't build big complex projects like mobile or web apps, softwares, dashboards, marketplaces, etc. Also, complex 3D modeling.

What softwares do you use?

We use Figma for pretty much all type of design work, because of it's wide range of tools and collaborative functionality. Then also Adobe Creative suite, when required. And Webflow for website development, which we're certified experts of.
